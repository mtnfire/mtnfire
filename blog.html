🔥 Proof

May 5, 2025

There’s a line in a song that says:
"You want proof that God is real? I’m standing here."

That line hit me like a freight train today.

I don’t need to convince you with doctrine or debate. I don’t need to pull out an argument.
I am the evidence.
I was broken. I was lost. I was tormented. I was bound.
But not anymore.

What you see now is a man on fire—not because I lit it, but because God lit it in me.
This isn’t religion. This isn’t performance.
This is what it looks like when Jesus shows up and does what no one else could.

I’m living proof that freedom is possible.
That healing is real.
That the fire of the Holy Spirit still falls.

If you’ve ever asked if God is real...
Look at me.

I’m not the same.
And I’ll never be the same again.

— Matt / MTN.fire
