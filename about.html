About Me

I’m Matt Culbertson—founder of MTN.fire. I’m a single dad, bass player, and straight-up Appalachian revivalist who’s walked through the fire and lived to testify. I’ve always had a heart for people and the truth, but I used to carry a lot of pain. After years of struggling through trauma, I found healing, clarity, and my calling in the middle of a Holy Spirit revival in 2025.

Now I’m here doing what God told me to do: sharing my story, my faith, and my fire with anyone who needs it. MTN.fire isn’t a trend or a brand gimmick—it’s my testimony in motion.

I still love tech, music, and good stories. And if you stick around, I think we’ll have a few to tell together.
